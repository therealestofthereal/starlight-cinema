About Starlight Cinema

More than a movie. An experience.

Who We Are

Starlight Cinema is more than a cinema. It is an experience created by cinephiles, for cinephiles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the ultimate cinema experience to as many people as possible with affordable pricing. Any other cinema? Can't bring your own food or drinks, popcorn is expensive. Starlight Cinema? Free popcorn with every ticket, food and drinks can be brought into the cinema (but if you leave a mess, you'll have to pay a fine.)

What Makes Us Different

  • Curated movie selections
  • Comfort-first seating
  • Late-night and themed screenings
  • Fair and transparent pricing

The Starlight Experience

From the moment the cinema first dims and the screen turns on and the film starts rolling to the moment the film ends, our surround sound experience and very comfortable seating will make sure that you will be engaged in the film. Loud neighbours, someone annoying you? Press the button on your seat, we'll deal with them for you.
